Welcome To Zia Ul Sunnah Educational and Charitable Trust(R.)

Markaz Zia Ul Sunnah is an Islamic Educational and Charitable Trust, committed to imparting Islamic knowledge based on the teachings of Ahl-e-Sunnat Wal Jama'aat.

Under the guidance and blessings of huzoor MUHADDIS E KABEER ALLAMA ZIA UL MUSTAFA QADRI Hafidahullah and Qazi-ul-Quzzat Fil Hind, Hazrat Allama Maulana Mufti MUHAMMAD ASJAD RAZA KHAN QADRI RAZAVI NOORI Hafidahullah, we are determined to make meaningful strides in our mission, transforming our humble efforts into a beacon of success.

Our institution provides comprehensive Islamic studies for both male and female students, fostering a deep understanding of Islamic values and knowledge.

Our Vision

Our vision is to spread lessions of Ahl-e-Sunnat Wal Jama'aat to the world.

Our Mission

Our misson is to spread true Islamic lessions from Quran and Hadiths
